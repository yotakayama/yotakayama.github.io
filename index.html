Yoshinari Takayama

Hi, I am Yoshinari Takayama, a PhD student at the Laboratoire des Signaux et Systèmes (L2S), a joint research unit of Centrale Supélec,Université Paris-Saclay and CNRS. My research focuses on the control and verification of cyber-physical systems using symbolic methods, with a particular emphasis on power systems applications. The thesis is funded by the French National Research Agency (ANR).

Between 2021 and 2023, I completed my master’s degree at the Graduate School of Informatics, Kyoto University, under the supervison of Toshiyuki Ohtsuka. Between 2017 and 2021, I completed my bachelor’s degree at the School of Informatics and Mathematical Science, Faculty of Engineering, Kyoto University.
